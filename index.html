Visit Iran

There is enough to see and do in the city for a day or two, but the best thing about Tehran is that it is spread out between two valleys, the mountain and the desert, and on the southern slopes of the Alborz mountain range.

Baghe Ferdos

Ferdous Garden of Tehran is considered one of the beautiful gardens of the capital, which has been remembered since the Qajar era. This garden, which is located near Tajrish Square, is dedicated to the cinema museum, and with facilities such as cinema halls, cafes and a pleasant and green environment, it is one of the hangouts of artists and art lovers.

Azadi Tower

Azadi Tower, which was known as Shahyad Tower before the 1357 revolution, is a structure located in Azadi Square in Tehran, Iran. Azadi Tower is the main symbol of Tehran and Tehran is known by this symbol almost everywhere in the world

Darband

Darband is an excellent area with a pleasant climate, where many residents of the capital seek refuge from the city's congestion, traffic and air pollution, to enjoy its incredibly beautiful landscape, and to enjoy a delicious meal in its numerous restaurants.
